# Switching on revenue

Set in Vercel (Settings → Environment Variables) or edit `src/data/config.ts`.

| Variable | What it is |
|---|---|
| `VITE_SITE_URL` | Live URL (used in share links + meta) |
| `VITE_TIP_URL` | Buy Me a Coffee / Ko-fi |
| `VITE_AFF_GEAR` | Affiliate link (design tools, courses) |
| `VITE_PRO_CHECKOUT` | Gumroad product for premium palette packs |
| `VITE_TWITTER_URL` / `VITE_GITHUB_URL` | Your profiles |

## How it earns
- **Tips + affiliate** from day one (designers/devs are a high-intent audience).
- **Pro packs**: curated palette collections, extra export formats, saved collections.

## Growth
Dev Twitter and r/web_design love color tools. Post striking palettes with the share link (the URL is the palette). "Palette of the day" posts compound.
